6 Steps To Installing Epoxy Coatings in St. Petersburg

1

Surface Preparation

We clean, grind, or shot blast the floor to remove debris, oil, & coatings ensuring proper adhesion.

2

Moisture & Substrate Testing

We check for moisture levels and substrate integrity to prevent future coating failures.

3

Primer Application

Apply a high-quality base epoxy specifically formulated to enhance adhesion and durability.

4

Base Coat & Decorative

Spread the body coat, add decorative flakes, pigments, or textures if desired.

5

Topcoat Application

Seal with a durable UV-resistant polyaspartic topcoat for chemical resistance and longevity.

6

Curing & Final Walkthrough

Allow proper curing time, then inspect for imperfections ensuring a smooth finish.
